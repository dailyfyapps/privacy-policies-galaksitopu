1. Information We Collect

Galaksi Topu does not directly collect or store any personal information. However, third-party services integrated into the app may collect certain data:

  • Google AdMob — collects device identifiers and usage data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. See Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Play Billing — purchase transactions are processed by Google. We do not store payment information.

2. Data Storage

Game progress such as levels completed, gems earned, and lives remaining is stored locally on your device only. No data is transmitted to our servers.

3. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

4. Advertising

This app displays advertisements via Google AdMob. Ads may be personalized based on your interests. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

5. Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

  • INTERNET — required for ads and billing
  • VIBRATE — for haptic feedback during gameplay
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS — for optional daily game reminders (Android 13+)
